About Landledger

Landledger addresses the challenges of land disputes, fraudulent transactions, and outdated record-keeping systems in Nigeria. By leveraging blockchain technology, Landledger ensures that land ownership records are immutable, transparent, and accessible to all stakeholders.

Key Features:

  • Secure and tamper-proof land records.
  • Transparent ownership transfers.
  • Easy access to land transaction history.
  • Reduction in land disputes and fraud.
